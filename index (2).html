About Us

Excellence in Healthcare

50+ Years of Excellence

Leading Healthcare Provider in Nandi County

Kapsabet County Referral Hospital is a Level 5 government hospital serving as the primary referral facility for Nandi County and surrounding regions. Established in 1965, we have grown to become a center of medical excellence.

Our hospital is equipped with modern medical technology and staffed by highly qualified healthcare professionals dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-centered care.

Our Vision

To be the leading referral hospital in Western Kenya, providing exceptional healthcare services that meet international standards and improve the quality of life for all our patients.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services through innovation, research, and continuous professional development while maintaining the highest ethical standards.
